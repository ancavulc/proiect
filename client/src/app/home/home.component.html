Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a question about a lesson before booking?

You can get in touch with the instructor directly if you have any questions about lessons prior to making a booking. To contact an instructor, go to his/her profile and check their phone number.

 Where do I meet my instructor?

You can arrange the meeting point with your chosen instructor by personally contacting him using the phone number displayed on each profile. We recommend agreeing this before you book your lessons, but it can be arranged at a later date. The instructor should be able to suggest somewhere that is convenient for you both.

 Can I cancel a booking with an instructor?

Yes, you can do this by personally contacting the instructor.

 How can I leave a review?

Go to the Instructors page, choose your instructor's profile and write a review.
